Gardening Tips & Care Guide

Gardening is both an art and a science. To keep your plants healthy:

  • Always choose the right pot with good drainage.
  • Water plants according to their specific needs.
  • Use high-quality soil and fertilizers to promote growth.
  • Provide adequate sunlight or shade depending on the plant type.
  • Regularly prune and remove dead leaves to maintain plant health.

By following these simple tips, you can ensure your garden stays vibrant and full of life throughout the year.
